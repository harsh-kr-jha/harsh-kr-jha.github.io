Faoud — Built Here
"I didn't come from a place that made it easy.
I came from a place that made it necessary."

Growing up in Purnea, Bihar, ambition had to be self-generated. There was no startup culture, no network of mentors, no visible path. But there was a particular kind of drive that small towns give you — the one that asks what's possible beyond the horizon.

That drive carried me to IIT Bombay, where I didn't just study — I built. Products used by thousands. Communities that shaped campus life. Systems that outlasted my time there. And then to Hilti Group, where I touched something different: the intersection of industrial hardware, large-scale software, and real-world manufacturing.

Now, all of it is channelled into Faoud — a startup that I believe is the most important thing I've worked on.

What It Is

A manufacturing intelligence platform — built to bring clarity, traceability, and systems-thinking to factory floors.

Manufacturing SMEs in India operate in opacity. Orders are tracked on WhatsApp. Quality decisions are made on instinct. Shop floors generate data that never becomes information. Faoud is infrastructure for this gap.

We are building tools for production tracking, quality control, and operational visibility — designed to meet manufacturers where they are, not where software vendors wish they were.

Faoud is not just a product. It is a perspective: that the future of Indian manufacturing runs through better software, not just better hardware.
